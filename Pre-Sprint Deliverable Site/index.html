Project Overview:

Team Name: Grassroot Designers

Our SMAD/CIS team will collaborate to deliver a product for the Old School Alumni Group, a grass roots, non-profit organization that seeks to stimulate and increase the growth and development of JMU African American students. This system will assist students in applying to internship and job opportunities and will also allow the Old School Alumni to actively track their current members and students to advise them on opportunities. Finally, this system will bolster the Old School Alumni Groupâ€™s LinkedIn presence and create a central hub where students can browse opportunities.

The scope of work for the SMAD side of this project will include: User Research, User Story Personas, Low-Fidelity Prototypes, Wireframes, and the final Bootstrap 5 functional site. The scope of work for the CIS side of this project will include Bcck/Front-End development using Visual Studio and Database Devlopement in SQL Management Studio.

Project Goals:

  • Develop a functional opportunity portal with no errors within the provided time frame.
  • Deliver a state of the art, easy to use interface and functional database for our clients. By working closely with the client, we hope to exceed all their expectations as our group will create a system that helps students find internship/job opportunities and helps companies find quality candidates within the organization.
  • Integrate this system with AWS to create a fully-functional web application.
  • Be a top 2 team in the Capstone Competition.

Timeline:

We are basing our project timeline on 3 set dates laid out for our sprint deliverables. Sprint 1 will be due on March 24th and is where 1/3rd of our functionality will be completed. Sprint 2 will be due on April 5th and is where 2/3rds of the functionality will be completed. Sprint 3 will be due on April 19th and is where all the functionality for our project should be completed. For every sprint we will meet with our client and present our current system so we can get a better understanding of all the requirements.

Obstacles / Potential Issues:

In addressing potential issues, being able to simultaneously fulfill clients expectations while also having a realistic understanding of the limits on our capabilities is an issue that may arise. Awareness of this possible issue will ensure we are managing the threat of scope creep, as well as giving the client an attainable expectation for the final product. There is the obstacle of managing a working group of this size. Due to the weight it puts on communication, and collaboration. To address this we emphasize meeting presence, and conduct scheduling utilizing When2meet.
